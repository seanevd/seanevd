About

I have a passion for making things. When I get passionate about an idea, I make it happen. I have been making websites since I first discovered that I could make one myself at 13. I currently work as a front end web developer at ICON Health and Fitness. I am currently in the process of redesigning their customer service websites to have a better user experience. Before ICON, I worked at USU as a web developer and helped with the transition of a new site design as well as other projects. I have been working, professionally, in the field of Web Development since early 2012.

Also, I am working on my B.S. in Management Information Systems and will finish in June. As part of my major I am learning things from accounting and project management to big data analytics and database management. I have a background in Graphic Design and have a passion for good design and code. I spend a good portion of my free time researching UI/UX design principles and making new pens on Codepen. I also love being with my wife, listening to music, cycling, inventing, and learning new things.
